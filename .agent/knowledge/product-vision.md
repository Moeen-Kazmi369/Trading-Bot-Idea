# Product Vision - TradingBotAI

## Mission
To empower traders with an evolving AI that decodes institutional market patterns, moving beyond static, leaked retail strategies.

## Core Problem
The "retail vs. institutional" dynamic creates a market where common, public strategies become predictable and are exploited by market controllers. Traditional trading bots fail because they are static and based on these same leaked strategies.

## Target Users
- **Primary:** The developer/trader (3 years experience) who understands market controller dynamics.
- **Goal:** Create a personal "weapon" for trading that adapts as fast as the market controllers do.

## Core Solution
An autonomous trading system that:
1.  **Extracts Insight:** Uses deep market experience as a starting point.
2.  **Validates Mathematically:** Backtests every pattern against massive historical data.
3.  **Discovers Autonomously:** Uses Gemini AI to find "interesting points" and crack new formulas for liquidating the liquidators.
4.  **Adapts 24/7:** Continuously monitors for strategy decay and replaces old patterns with new ones.

## Not in Scope
- **Sentiment/News Trading:** All signals must come from raw price action data (OHLCV).
- **Public Strategy Copying:** We don't copy indicators; we decode the "cause" of price movement.
- **Day 1 Live Trading:** No real money until the Testnet has proven the system statistically.
